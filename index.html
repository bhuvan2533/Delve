Namma Bengaluru

Bengaluru also called as Bangalore refers to as Garden City of India due to its wide range of gardens and parks throughout the city. Today, Bangalore is a home of many well-recognized colleges and research centres, private industries, software companies, telecommunications, defence organizations, aerospace, etc. Bangalore is also called as the Silicon Valley of India due to its nation leading position in IT Market.
